# StyleNirm ✨ — Your AI Personal Stylist

> *Never stress about what to wear again. StyleNirm suggests your complete head-to-toe look every day — clothes, shoes, jewellery, bags and accessories — all matched perfectly.*

---

## 💡 The Problem I Solved

Every morning I was spending **20-30 minutes** just deciding what to wear to the office. With over 100 clothing items, shoes, earrings and accessories, matching everything felt overwhelming. I wanted to always look put-together and professional without the daily stress.

So I built **StyleNirm** — my own personal AI stylist that lives on my phone!

---

## ✨ What StyleNirm Does

StyleNirm is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** — meaning it installs on your phone like a real app, with no App Store needed.

Every day, you tell it:
- 📅 What's the occasion (office, meeting, dinner, party)
- 🌤️ What the weather is like
- 💫 What style vibe you want (elegant, minimal, bold, classic)

And the **AI suggests your complete look:**
- 👗 Which dress, top or outfit to wear
- 👠 Which shoes match
- 💍 Which earrings and jewellery complement it
- 👜 Which bag to carry
- 🧣 Any extras like scarves or belts

## 📱 How to Install on Your Phone

StyleNirm is a PWA — no App Store needed!

**Android:**
1. Open the live link in Chrome
2. Tap the 3-dot menu (⋮)
3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
4. Done! App icon appears on your home screen ✅

**iPhone:**
1. Open the live link in Safari
2. Tap the Share button
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Done! ✅

## 🤖 How the AI Works

When you tap "Create My Complete Look":
1. Your wardrobe list is sent to **Claude AI** (Anthropic)
2. Claude reads your clothes, occasion, weather and style vibe
3. It picks specific items that work together
4. Returns a complete styled outfit with explanation
5. You can save it to your weekly planner!

*Powered by Claude Sonnet via Anthropic API*

## 🧠 Product Thinking

### Problem
Working women with large wardrobes spend significant time every morning deciding what to wear — leading to decision fatigue, stress and sometimes arriving late.

### Target User
Working women aged 20-35 who care about looking professional and put-together but want to save time in their morning routine.

### Solution
An AI-powered wardrobe manager that learns your clothes and suggests complete, occasion-appropriate looks — removing the daily decision entirely.

### Key Decisions Made
- **PWA over native app** — No App Store needed, instant install, works on all phones
- **Local storage over cloud** — Maximum privacy, works offline, no login required
- **Complete look over just clothes** — Users need shoes + jewellery suggestions too, not just clothing
- **Weekly planner** — Plan outfits the night before to eliminate morning stress entirely

### Future Improvements
- [ ] Automatic weather detection using location
- [ ] Outfit rating system — track what you loved wearing
- [ ] "Not worn recently" alerts — rotate your full wardrobe
- [ ] Share your look on social media
- [ ] Seasonal wardrobe sorting
- [ ] Laundry tracker — know what's clean and available
